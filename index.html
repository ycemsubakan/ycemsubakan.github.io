Y. Cem Subakan

 

Y. Cem Subakan, Postdoctoral Researcher
Mila, Quebec Institute of Artificial Intelligence

I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Bogazici University EE department, respectively in 2011 and 2013. In Bogazici, I worked with A. Taylan Cemgil and Bulent Sankur. I got my PhD from Computer Science Department of University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My PhD advisor was Paris Smaragdis. In Mila, I work with Laurent Charlin.

Representative papers
